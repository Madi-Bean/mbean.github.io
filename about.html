About Our Team

At Pet Rentals, our priorities lie with our animals. We ensure that each one is given an opportunity to make new friends and ensure their comfort and stability. We also understand that not all pet lovers are capable of permanently keeping pets, while still wanting to occasionally enjoy the experience of having a pet in the home. Our mission is:

  1. To make pet ownership more accessible
  2. To foster greater relationships between people and animals
  3. To reduce life's stresses through animal therapy

We believe in the healing power of animals and understand that while not everyone has the resources to keep pets, everyone should have the opportunity to interact with them.
